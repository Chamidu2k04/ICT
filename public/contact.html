Frequently Asked Questions

When are the classes held?

Classes are held every Sunday at 7 PM. All sessions are live-recorded and available for later viewing.

Are the classes really free?

Yes, absolutely! All A-Level ICT classes are completely free as part of my commitment to accessible education.

Who can join the classes?

The classes are primarily designed for the 2027 A-Level batch, but anyone interested in ICT education is welcome to join.

How can I access past recordings?

All recorded sessions are available on our YouTube channel. You can access them anytime for revision.
