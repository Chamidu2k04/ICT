About Me

Hello Everyone!

My name is Chamidu Lakshan. I am a university student currently pursuing a BSc Honours in Information Technology - specializing in Software Engineering at SLIIT. I am currently in my second year.

I wish to start tuition classes after a few years. Before that, I want to gain hands-on experience with students. So, I have decided to teach free, complete advanced level Information and Communication Technology lessons for students. The lucky batch is the 2027 A-Level batch.

Additionally, I have already completed Grade 6 to Grade 11 complete lessons, term test papers, and past paper discussions. These are summarized videos meant as revision and past paper discussions, not full teaching lessons.

My Mission

In the A-Level ICT series, I share live-recorded video classes with my students.

Everyone is welcome to learn! My main goal is to provide quality education while improving my teaching skills.

I am open to hearing your comments — good, bad, or otherwise — because my main goal is to improve myself before starting tuition classes.

What I Offer

Live A-Level Classes

Complete A-Level ICT lessons delivered live every Sunday at 7:00 PM. Interactive sessions where students can ask questions and get immediate feedback.

Revision Materials

Comprehensive Grade 6-11 revision videos, term test papers, and past paper discussions to help students prepare effectively for exams.

Free Classes

Some classes and materials are provided completely free of charge. My goal is to make quality ICT education accessible to everyone.

A Personal Note

This website was created by me and is currently under construction. I hope to complete it within the upcoming years as I continue to learn and grow.

Thank you everyone for your support and for being part of this learning journey. Together, we can achieve great things!
