About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Chamidu Lakshan. I am a university student currently pursuing a BSc Honours in Information Technology - specialising in Software Engineering at SLIIT. I am currently in my second year.


I wish to start tuition classes after a few years. Before that, I want to gain hands-on experience with students. So, I have decided to teach free, complete advanced level Information and Communication Technology lessons for students. The lucky batch is the 2027 A-Level batch.


Additionally, I have already completed Grade 6 to Grade 11 complete lessons, term test papers, and past paper discussions. These are summarised videos meant as revision and past paper discussions, not full teaching lessons.


In the A-Level ICT series, I share live-recorded video classes with my students. Everyone is welcome to learn!


I am open to hearing your comments — good, bad, or otherwise — because my main goal is to improve myself before starting tuition classes.


This website was created by me and is currently under construction. I hope to complete it within the upcoming years.

Thank you everyone again!
