About Me

Passionate educator dedicated to free ICT education

Hello, I'm Chamidu Lakshan

I am a university student currently pursuing a BSc Honours in Information Technology - specialising in Software Engineering at SLIIT. I am currently in my second year, passionate about technology and education.

My Mission

I wish to start tuition classes after a few years. Before that, I want to gain hands-on experience with students. So, I have decided to teach free, complete advanced level Information and Communication Technology lessons for students. The lucky batch is the 2027 A-Level batch.

What I Offer

I have already completed Grade 6 to Grade 11 complete lessons, term test papers, and past paper discussions. These are summarised videos meant as revision and past paper discussions, not full teaching lessons.

In the A-Level ICT series, I share live-recorded video classes with my students. Everyone is welcome to learn!

Feedback Welcome

I am open to hearing your comments — good, bad, or otherwise — because my main goal is to improve myself before starting tuition classes. Your feedback helps me become a better educator.
